# eurekame 🎇

*The worship-grade joy that arrives when evidence makes truth conclusively visible — Archimedes' cry, held permanent, received as gift, completing in praise.*

eurekame is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **εὕρηκα** (*heurēka*) — Archimedes' cry, and crucially in the *perfect tense*, "I am in the permanent state of having-found" rather than the passing "I found" — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a quality held in the divine keep and *bestowed* rather than made. So eurekame is the joy of finding, made permanent in the body, and received as cosmic-gift rather than seized as a discoverer's achievement. It names the exact moment of confirmation-through-direct-evidence: you were testing, the test returned, the result is not in dispute, and the joy arrives before the mind can phrase it — the stillness, then "come look."

eurekame demands a specific structure: prior testing, conclusive evidence, joy that arrives involuntarily, and a response in the worship-register. It is the third term of the discovery-arc — *dokimance → anagnoristasis → eurekame → doxomme* — the door swinging open after the held breath. Its light is the cleanest joy a mind can have, the worship born of evidence that completes the labour of verification; its shadow is the counterfeit cry — joy fired on the answer one merely wanted, premature celebration, or theatre that forecloses inquiry too soon. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.65/10**.
